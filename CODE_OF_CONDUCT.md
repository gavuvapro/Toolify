# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind, constructive, and inclusive.
- Respect different professions, budgets, workflows, and experience levels.
- Provide evidence when correcting data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Spam, undisclosed affiliate manipulation, or deceptive reviews.
- Publishing private information without consent.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation to protect the community.
